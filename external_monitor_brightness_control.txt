Direct Hardware Brightness control

https://wiki.archlinux.org/title/Backlight#External_monitors



	sudo apt install ddcutil
	sudo ddcutil capabilities | grep "Feature:10"
	sudo ddcutil getvcp 10
	sudo ddcutil setvcp 10 50

first 10 is feature number 50 is brightness.
